About The River Run

The River Run sources naturally caught fish from rivers, seas, canals and beels across Bangladesh. We work directly with local fishermen to bring fresh fish to urban homes.

Our goal is simple — deliver clean, fresh and authentic fish to your family with reliable delivery and honest sourcing.
