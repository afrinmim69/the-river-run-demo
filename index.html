How We Source Fish

The River Run partners with fishing communities from Padma, Meghna and coastal regions of Bangladesh. Every morning our sourcing team collects fish directly from fishermen and preserves them in ice boxes. The fish are transported to our packing point and delivered safely to customers ensuring freshness and quality.
